Information

Born and raised in France, Dylan left his hometown after high school in order to study Film in Los Angeles, CA. Art always being his passion, he turned it into a professional career at a young age. He now works in the selective field of international fashion photographers, being based in the US and Europe.

Dylan Perlot's work has been published in numerous well-known publications such as: Vogue Italia, Vogue Ukraine, Harper's Bazaar, Cosmopolitan, Marie Claire, L’Officiel, V Magazine, Glamour, Numéro, Gay Times, Nylon, Schön!, Grazia etc. He also had the honor to photograph talented artists like Daphne Guinness, Alok, Julia Michaels and Nicky Doll to name a few.

In 2020 and 2023, his multiple photography projects received international recognition with notable awards to add to the worldwide success of his experimental fashion films “Life” in 2016 and “Intruder” in 2019. In addition, his photo series "United" had the honor of being exhibited in Barcelona, Spain during "Panoràmic Festival" in late 2020.

Creating has always been what he loves to do the most, and combining many elements such as: people, fashion, and places are his best way to tell stories. His inspiration comes from different kinds of arts mixed with memories from childhood spent in Europe and new life culture in America. It helped him shape his own photography style known to be unique for its edge, sharpness and softness.
